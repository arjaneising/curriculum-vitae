Profile

I'm a devotee of everything related to client side web development. As front-end web developer I have over seven years of professional experience on various medium and large scale websites and applications.

Semantics, coding style, readable code and performance. It is what I stand for and fight for on a project.

I started writing HTML when I just left primary education. During the time on secondary education, I started my own business. Shortly after graduating pre-university education, I co-founded the Dutch trade organization for front-end developers: Fronteers.

New tools and languages are very important for me. It is not good to stick with something if there are other, better ones available.

Well argumented critiscism can count on a proper response from me. I like to work in a team where the bars are set high, and can comment on other people theirs code in the same way I like to get it.
